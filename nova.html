My Daily Musings

June 15, 2023

Why do we care so much about what others think?

Arey yaar, sach bolu toh ye sab hum sabko hota hai. Even mujhe hota tha—still hota hai kabhi-kabhi.
Ek baar Class 7 mein, Annual Day dance tha. Main full josh mein thi, like outfit ready, steps on point... confidence max! Par fir ek din kisi ne bola, "Nova padhai mein acchi hai but dance mein thodi awkward hai na." Bas. Kya hi bolu. Uske baad mera poora vibe hi down ho gaya. Stage pe bhi, instead of enjoying, main bas ye soch rahi thi—“Log dekh rahe honge... kya soch rahe honge...”
Aur tab samjha ki hum itna overthink isliye karte hain kyunki hum sab ko lagta hai ki approval milna zaroori hai. School life mein har jagah comparison hota hai—marks, looks, Instagram followers, everything! Toh subconsciously hum bas logon ko impress karne mein lag jaate hain, instead of living freely.
But brooo—logon ki opinion literally weather jaise hoti hai. Aaj kuch aur, kal kuch aur. Tumhare baare mein kya socha, kabhi yaad bhi nahi rehta unko. Par hum apne dimaag mein us thought ko permanent rent pe de dete hain!
Ab toh scene simple hai—do your thing. Apne standards set karo. Jo pasand hai, karo. Galti hogi, log bolenge, but at least tum real rahoge. And trust me—jab tum khud pe proud feel karte ho, na logon ki unnecessary baatein automatically mute ho jaati hain.
So chill kar, kaam kar, and apni vibe high rakh!
